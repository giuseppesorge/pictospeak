# Caregiver and family guide

*(Draft — will be completed when the app is demo-ready. Translations of this guide will
ship with each language pack.)*

PictoSpeak is an AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) app: the person composes
a message by tapping pictograms, the app proposes a correct sentence, **the person confirms
it**, and the device speaks it aloud. Everything works without internet and without any
account: no data ever leaves the device.

## One-time setup

1. **Offline voice**: on first launch the app checks that the device has a voice for your
   language that works without a connection. If it is missing, the app guides you through
   the download (a connection is needed only for this one step).
2. **Profile**: grid size (from 3×2 up to 6×10), optional spoken preview of single words,
   voice and speech rate.

## Daily use

- Tap pictograms: they appear in the strip at the top.
- The app proposes a sentence; tapping it cycles through the alternatives.
- Only the confirm button makes the device speak: **the app never speaks on its own**.
