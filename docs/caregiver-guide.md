# Caregiver and family guide

*(Draft — will grow as features land. Translations ship with each language pack.)*

PictoSpeak is a pictogram communication app: the person composes a message by tapping
pictograms, the app proposes a correct sentence, **the person confirms it**, and the device
speaks it aloud. Everything works without internet and without any account — no data ever
leaves the device.

## One-time setup

On first launch the app checks the speech engine and shows a **speech setup** screen:

- **An offline voice is ready** — nothing to do; the device speaks without internet.
- **A voice must be downloaded** — tap *Install voice data* (this one step needs internet).
  Do it once, during setup, while online.
- **No voice / no engine** — open *Settings*, try another language, or install a text-to-speech
  engine from the device's app store.

Use *Test voice* to hear it, then *Continue*.

## Daily use

- Tap pictograms: they appear in the strip at the top.
- The app proposes a sentence; tap it to cycle through alternatives (for example a different
  article or tense).
- **Only the Speak button makes the device talk.** The app never speaks on its own.
- *Backspace* removes the last pictogram; *Clear* empties the message.

## Settings (caregiver)

Open the info button, then **press and hold** "Caregiver settings" (a deliberate gate so the
person cannot open settings by accident). There you can set:

- **Language** — Italian or English. Changing it switches the whole board, the grammar, and
  the voice.
- **Speech rate** and **pitch** — a slower rate is often clearer.
- **Speak word when tapped** — hear each pictogram's word as it is tapped.
- **Backup** — *Export settings* saves a small file you can keep or share; *Import settings*
  restores it on another device.

## Frequently asked questions

- **Does it need internet?** No — only the one-time voice download during setup.
- **Is my data private?** Yes. Nothing is sent anywhere; there is no account and no analytics.
- **The device is silent.** Open the speech setup / Settings and check the voice; install the
  offline voice for your language.
